riskMeasures.GenCoherent — Method

Coherent risk measure Calculates a general coherent risk measure based on duality representations

for coherent risk measures the conditions E(Z) = 1 and Z ≥ 0 are enforced.
conds needs to be a real valued function F such that F(Z) ≤ 0.
To obtain a coherent risk measure F should be positively homegeneous.
source

riskMeasures.mSD — Method

An implementation of the Mean semi-deviation risk measure of order p. This is a coherent risk measure which can be nested by adjusting the risk level β → β⋅√(Δt)

mSD_β^p(Y) = EY + β ⋅ ||(Y-EY)_+||_p
